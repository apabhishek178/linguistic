THE LINGUISTIC COMMITTEE


Based in College Of Engineering Roorkee(COER), The Linguisitic Committee aims at promulgating language-based knowledge and providing with a platform that aims at enhancing the soft-skills of the students. The Linguistic Committee was introduced under the leadership of Dr. Rashmi Gupta and Ms. Divya in the year 2006. Prof. Shashi Choudhary later joined the committee as the Co-convener till 2015.
Currently Dr. Sushil Jindal is the co-conveners of The Linguistic Committee. This committee has been serving the purpose of ameliorating a studentâ€™s personality for a little over a decade.
The focal point of our committee is Expression, our annual college fest. The fest has various events to test students' skills. All the events revolve around the fest-theme which is refashioned every year. The events are set in a manner which test an individual's speaking skills, writing skills, debating skills, knowledge of English grammar, knowledge of current affairs, thinking out of the box abilities and perspicacity. There are also fun events for students to have a gala time.
